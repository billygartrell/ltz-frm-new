Voted best farmers market of 2011-

The market is open air seasonal market located on North Water Street 1/2 block off of East Main Street in downtown Lititz. Featuring a full variety of locally produced products sold by the farmers in a festive setting.

More about the market:

Live music, children's activities, chef demonstrations and a community interest table make the Lititz Farmers Market a family friendly way to spend a Saturday morning. The farmers bring fresh vegetables, fruits, baked goods, dairy, meats, eggs, jellies, flowers, plants and much more. Shoppers can leave the market with everything needed for a special Saturday night dinner or just a week of delicious food.

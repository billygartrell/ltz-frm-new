Lititz is a vibrant, bustling town

When you visit Lititz, prepare for a unique and unforgettable experience. Our downtown attractions – and those just beyond our town limits – offer a wide variety of opportunities for fun, fascination and adventure!

Places to go after you visit the market:

  • Wilbur Chocolate Factory
  • Lititz Springs Park
  • Lititz Historical Foundation and Museum
  • Julius Sturgis Pretzel Factory
  • Lititz Train Station
